Curtains And Blinds
Windows (and access to natural light) can make or break a space, but many people overlook the importance of window treatments while planning a space. Window coverings can be functional, purely decorative, or strike a balance between the two, depending on your space and the amount of natural light you receive and prefer. Whether it's full and flowing drapes, modern coverings, classic curtains, Roman shades, or laidback shutters, the ideas ahead are sure to inspire a better view at home.
Curtain systems have two important roles, they enhance design whilst managing the light and heat entering a room. Annapoornaa interio curtain tracks are designed to have outstanding gliding properties, with a choice of systems that can accommodate the lightest to the heaviest of curtains.
The wide range of tracks ensure there is a profile design and color that will fit with any interior design scheme. Cord-operated tracks are suited to heavy or inaccessible curtains and avoid the need to touch the curtain fabric. Our tracks can be bent for bay windows and other curved applications. Systems are available for all fabric weights, including heavier, lined curtains.
Your modern home decor will never be the same with little change in curtains just the color and fabric make a huge difference.
