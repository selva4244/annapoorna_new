Commercial Buildings Construction
Commercial Construction means the Building Projects are to be leased- out or sold in the Private Sector on completion. Office Buildings, Factory Buildings, Medical Shops and Retail. Shopping Complexes fall into this category.
Office Buildings are a common type of commercial building normally found in cities and towns of any size depending on the local needs of the respective communities.

These may include Restaurants, Retail Shops, Hotels, Warehouses, Industries, Health Care Units, and Multi Purpose Complexes. Commercial structure means any non-residential structure which may include any structure other than a one-to - four family dwelling , apart from shopping centers ,Offices, Nursing Homes and other similar structures.

Commercial construction being undertaken by business establishments include office buildings, Industrial facilities and other places of business. Residential constructions on the other hand are places where people live, such as houses, apartments and other homes.
